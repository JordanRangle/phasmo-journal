Cursed Items

Each map now contains 1 of 6 cursed items. Each cursed item spawns at a specific location on each map.

  1. Haunted Mirror

    Rotates in a 360 circle showing you the ghosts' room. The mirror drains 7%~ sanity per second. After a few seconds of using the mirror, the mirror will break triggering a hunt.

  2. Music Box

    If you are in or near the ghosts room, you will hear the ghost "sing". After 7 seconds or when the ghost reaches you, a hunt will start.

  3. Ouija Board

    Used to communiate with the ghost at the cost of sanity.

  4. Summoning Circle

    Summons and locks the ghost into the circle for 2 seconds, then starts a hunt.

  5. Tarot Cards

    Each deck inludes 10 random cards. Each card can be 1 of 10 cards, each with unique effects.

  6. Voodoo Doll

    Using the voodoo doll forces the ghost to preform an interaction. Useful when trying to trigger finger prints or blowing out a candel. When the pin in the voodoo doll's heart is pushed in, an hunt is triggered.
